Sample Data & Queries

Based on exercise questions

  • The Big Bang Theory Dataset - schema.org based dataset with schema:knows relations based on characters of The Big Bang Theory TV series.
  • Federal Statistical Office & Swisstopo
    • Get a specific shape in Swisstopo
    • Get specific municipalities at FSO
    • Federated query combining the two
  • Gujer Fotos Stadtarchiv Uster - A simple georeferenced RDF dataset containing historic pictures from Swiss photographer Julius Gujer
  • Language filtering based on sample data in DIDOK list. Try it on the SPARQL endpoint.
    • Graph: https://linked.opendata.swiss/graph/FOT/didok>
    • Tram
    • Funicular
  • Wikidata: Get airport codes, labels & location
